About

I started this site because I wanted to be able to share reviews and recommendations with other coeliacs and sufferers of gluten allergies and intolerances.

Eating out can be awkward for people with a gluten-free diet as some restaurants are hopeless! Fortunately, many are getting clued up and it seems to be getting easier to find good quality gluten-free places to eat.

I want to promote gluten friendly restaurants - those who understand our needs and can accommodate us. But I can't do it on my own - can you write a review?

For Businesses

It's essential that you that gluten-free requests are taken seriously and answered accurately, as many coeliacs can become extremely ill from small exposure. Others are less sensitive; good information is key.

Contact

I'd love to add your restaurant experiences to the site! Please send any reviews, recommendations, general feedback or ideas my way and I'll incorporate it as soon as possible.

It's really useful if you can include this kind of detail in your review:

  • Were they able to provide a gluten-free menu?
  • Were the staff clued up on what gluten-free means?
  • Did they have a good selection of gluten-free food?
